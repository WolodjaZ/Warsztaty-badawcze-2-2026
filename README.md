## 📅 Course Structure
### Week 1 (03.03): Hello Everyone

- Get to know the course
- Talk about the projects
- I understand that some of you are not familiar with Deep Learning, **so please just ask me** if you have any questions. I don't want people to feel lost during the course and be afraid of the projects.
- Your task fro the next two weeks will be to choose the group of **2-3** people and the topic.

### Week 2 (08.03): The touch of modern AI
On site we will get to know how to do basic staff with AI libraries like pytorch or huggingface and we will do everything on colab the place where you can get free GPU wich will be needed.
- Task: On site programing exercise

### Week 3 (15.03): The touch of modern AI - part 2
The final exercise will be to use pretrained models to train a simple classifier and next finetune a pretrained model on a specific dataset.
- Task: Untill the end of the day you will have to do an exercise on colab and submit the link to the notebook with the descriptions of the steps you took to do the task. (I will be on site to help you if you have any questions)
- Before the lessons I get the list of the groups and the topics they want to work on. I will try to help you choose the topic if you have any doubts.

### Week 4 (22.03): Paper presentation
Starting from this week, every week one group will present a paper related to the topic they choose. The presentation should be around 20 minutes long and should cover the main points of the paper, the methodology used, and the results obtained. The presentation should be followed by a discussion where other students can ask questions and share their thoughts on the paper (5 min). The presentation should follow this [rules](paper_presentation_rules.md). The first presentation I will do myself to show you how it should look like. The spreadsheet with the schedule of the presentations will be shared on the lesson.

## Grading Criteria

- Laboratory exercises: 0-10
- Paper presentation: 0-10
- Project work:
    - First milestone: 0-5 points based on the progress and quality of the work.
    - Second milestone: 0-10 points based on the progress and quality of the work.
    - Final milestone: 0-5 points based on the final implementation, results, and presentation.
- Project presentations: 0-7 points
  - Content: 0-2 points
  - Clarity: 0-2 points
  - Visuals: 0-2 points
  - Interaction with audience: 0-1 points
- Code Quality: 0-3 points
  - Readability: 0-1 points
  - Reproducibility: 0-1 points
  - Documentation: 0-1 points

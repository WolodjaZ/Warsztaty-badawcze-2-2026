## 📅 Course Roadmap (Summer 2026)

### Week 1 (27.02): Hello Everyone

* Course intro & project brainstorming.
* Goal: Form groups of **2-3** and start browsing the [Project Topics](project_description.md).

### Week 2 (06.03): The touch of modern AI

* Intro to PyTorch and HuggingFace via Google Colab. We will cover the basics of loading datasets, models and using Google Colab for training.
* Task: On-site programming exercise (Basics).

### Week 3 (13.03): The touch of modern AI - part 2

* Finetuning pretrained models and classification. The exercise will be to use pretrained models to train a simple classifier and next finetune a pretrained model on a specific dataset.
* Task: Submit Colab notebook link by EOD.
* Deadline: Finalize groups and topics.

### Week 4 (20.03): Paper presentation

Starting from this week, everyone will present a paper related to the project topic they have chosen. The presentation should be around 20 minutes long and should cover the main points of the paper, the methodology used, and the results obtained. The presentation should be followed by a discussion where other students can ask questions and share their thoughts on the paper (5 min). The presentation should follow this [rules](paper_presentation_rules.md). The first presentation I will do myself to show you how it should look like. The spreadsheet with dataset and later papers will be shared in the Slack channel.

### Week 5 (27.03): First project milestone

### Week 6-8: Project work, paper presentation, and consultations

### Week 9 (03.05): Second project milestone

### Week 10-12: Project work, paper presentation, and consultations

### Week 13 (29.05): Final project milestone

### Week 14 (03.06): Final project presentation

Each group will present their project in front of the class. The presentation should cover the motivation behind the project, the methodology used, the results obtained, and the conclusions drawn from the work. The presentation should be around 15 minutes long, followed by a discussion where other students can ask questions and share their thoughts on the project (5 min). The presentation will be graded based on content, clarity, visuals, and interaction with the audience.

### Week 15 (12.06): Bonus paper presentation

To obtain the bonus points, you can present an additional paper related to your project topic. The presentation should follow the same rules as the regular paper presentations. The deadline for the bonus presentation announcement is the end of Week 14 (07.06). The total number of points you can earn from the bonus presentation is 5 points, which will be added to your final project score.
